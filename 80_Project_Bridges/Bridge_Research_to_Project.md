This bridge defines how mature Research may transition into Project Prep and later into a real project.

## Entry into Project Prep

Move work into:
- `00_Artifacts/40_project-prep/`

when the research has enough gravity that implementation planning or structured project preparation makes sense.

## Typical progression

- Discovery
- Research
- Requirements
- optional Spec
- Implementation Plan
- separate project/repo when the work gains real project gravity

## Traceability rule

Project Prep artifacts should point back to the originating Discovery and Research artifacts.

## Human-in-the-loop rule

By default, movement from Research into Project Prep is human-in-the-loop unless a future workflow explicitly automates that decision.

## Important note

Not all Research should become projects.
Some should remain as backlog, reusable knowledge, or tutorial input.
